About Allied Medical Services

Trusted by families across Nigeria for professional embalming and safe body transportation.

For over 20 years, Allied Medical Services has helped families honor their loved ones with dignity, compassion, and professionalism. Our team ensures safe and respectful handling from the moment we are contacted, offering 24/7 service for embalming and body transportation across Nigeria.

Losing a loved one is never easy. At Allied Medical Services, we understand the pain and the need for dignity during this difficult time. That’s why we’ve dedicated over 20 years to providing professional embalming services and reliable ambulance support for families across Nigeria.

Our mission is simple: to give families the peace of mind that comes from knowing their loved ones are in safe hands. From carefully preserving the body to transporting it respectfully, every step of our service is carried out with professionalism, care, and cultural understanding.
