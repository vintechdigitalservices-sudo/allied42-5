Embalming is a process used to preserve the body of a loved one after death, especially when the family needs time for funeral arrangements, viewing, or transportation. It helps keep the body in a good and presentable condition before burial.

At Allied Medical Services Ltd, we carry out embalming with care, dignity, and respect. We understand that this is a painful time for families, so every step is handled gently and professionally.

Many families choose embalming so they can see their loved one one last time, say their goodbyes, and properly honor their life. Seeing the body helps many people accept the loss and begin the journey of healing.

What Our Embalming Service Includes

  • Proper cleaning and preparation of the body
  • Preservation to slow decomposition
  • Careful handling to maintain a natural appearance
  • Clean and hygienic procedures
  • Preparation for burial, viewing, or transportation

Why Families Choose Our Embalming Services

  • Gives families time to plan funeral rites
  • Allows loved ones to view the body
  • Supports long-distance transportation when needed
  • Handled by trained and experienced professionals
